About the project

The world of Hᴀᴛᴇ Cʏᴄʟᴇ is one of inherent magic and wonder, even if hidden underneath many layers of fear, oppression and hate. It’s a world of dragons, goblins, mages and dieties that hide from humanity out of fear of capture and containment. A world where all of your most wonderful dreams can come true, but you can’t talk about it, or else you will be silenced.

Something has left a deep scar that hasn’t fully healed yet. Something cut off the wings of fairies, something made aliens flee back to their planets. A terrible cataclysm rained down into the cracks and crevices of this world and stories are passed down mouth to mouth, parent to children, warning of the dangers of merely being.

But even then, every once in a while, something makes its way through; a woman in Wales meets a dragon; someone has a demonic experience; a man adopts a child with magical powers; little by little, this world is healing, and each and every story told here is a step into that direction.


About the author

Affur Akmosh! I'm Lusca Sharktopus, author of the stories you’ll read, watch, listen to and play on this website!
I've always loved drawing and creating stories since I was a kid; I dreamed of being the author of animated
series and used to draw comic books. I enjoy creating worlds, languages, speculating about the evolution of
organisms, and designing magic systems – Worldbuilding is easily my favorite part of the process – and in
recent years I’ve been truly invested in it, creating in 2021 the Hᴀᴛᴇ Cʏᴄʟᴇ universe, a shared universe where
I can tell (almost) all of my stories, united in a single world!

In addition, I’m a Biology major, a graphic designer, and I create all the covers and logos for the stories here
myself.
I’m also Art Director at Safe Haven, a project focused on telling stories through Tabletop RPG!
