About the author

Affur Akmosh! I'm Lusca Sharktopus, author of the stories you’ll find on this website!
I've always loved drawing and creating stories since I was a kid; I dreamed of being the author of animated series and used to draw comic books. I enjoy creating worlds, languages, speculating about the evolution of organisms, and designing magic systems – Worldbuilding is easily my favorite part of the process – and in recent years I’ve been truly invested in it, creating in 2021 the Hᴀᴛᴇ Cʏᴄʟᴇ universe, a shared universe where I can tell (almost) all of my stories, united in a single world!

In addition, I’m a Biology major, a graphic designer, and I create all the covers and logos for the stories here myself.
I’m also Art Director at Safe Haven, a project focused on telling stories through Tabletop RPG!
